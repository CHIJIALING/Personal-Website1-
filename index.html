something about the USA

At the age of 6, I started to learning English by reading the book passed down from my sister. Then, I just somehow fell in love with not only the language itself but also the American culture or maybe lifesyle. Through listening Miley Cyrus' songs, watching Hannah Montana as well as other series on Disney Channel, I was slightly influenced by the information it displayed. At the age of 12, it was my first time went to the USA, the place that I had longed for going to for so long. After going to this mind-liberal country, I love this place and its culture more and more. When I was 18, my parents allowed me to go to America alone after GSAT. Even though been through so many years, the feelings didn't change a bit, everything's still amazing and joyful to me~
